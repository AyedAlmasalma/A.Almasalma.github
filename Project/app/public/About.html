About

DNG Decoder

DNG Decoder is a student project developed for the purposes of my Bachelor's thesis and also a bit due to general curiosity. The webpage and thesis were made in the period between end Juli and middle of December 2023. The whole project is based on the small amount of information about DNG that can be found on the internet, seeing as there is only one official public documentation from Adobe. It isn't perfect and there are things that are missing. That is why a lot of research and experimentation was necessary from my part in order to find the solutions to the problems. The project itself uses pure JavaScript and framework vue.js for the whole decoding and processing. Overall I am very happy with the results of the project but there are always things that can be improved. There are some plans to update it later as a side project but for proper evaluation of the whole project there are no foreseen update until I have been graded on it and have graduated.
